What makes Tropex Steel unique

Tropex's steel products mainly include Carbon and low alloy high-strength steel, Boiler and pressure vessel steel, Gas cylinder steel, Shipbuilding and offshore platform steel, Pipeline steel, Weather resistant steel / corten steel, Alloy steel, Automobile structure steel, Abrasion Resistant steel, etc. It can execute ASTM, ASME, AISI, SAE, API, EN, DIN, ISO, JIS, GB and other international universal standards.

Winning global customer recognition

Tropex Steel has obtained ISO 9001: 2008, Class 10 Classification Society (ABS, BV, LR, DNV GL, CCS, RINA, NK, KR, RMRS), TUV, API and other certifications. With high-quality steel products and professional services, it has won international acclaim in the fields of infrastructure, shipbuilding, petroleum industry and gradually grew into a bright star in the steel export industry.
